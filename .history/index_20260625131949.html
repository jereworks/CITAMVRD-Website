Foundational Pillars

The C-I-T-A-M Core Values

How we live, serve, and fulfill our calling within our structural ministry ecosystems.

C

Community

We Uphold family,
value partnership,
and embrace unity.

I

Integrity

Live in holiness,
pursue excellence,
and practice honesty.

T

Transformation

Individual - Intentional discipleship,
Institutional - servant leadership,
Societal - Advocacy and Social Action.

A

Accountability

To God,
To one another,
and To government.

Ministry

To the lost - Grace and Mercy,
To the least - Love and Compassion,
and To the last - Dignity and Respect.

Our Ministries

From children to senior citizens, CITAM Valley Road operates structured ministry tracks engineered to build holistic, Christ-like disciples.

Men Ministry

Spurring men to command generational influence, lead godly family altars, and model professional integrity.

Women Ministry

Empowering and mentoring women across all life seasons through unified biblical truths and community.

Youth Ministry

A radical community transforming culture, accelerating innovation, and growing together in Christ.

Teens Ministry

Guiding the next generation with purpose, providing mentorship during their formative years.

Children Ministry

Intentionally parenting a foundational generation through structured Sunday school and creative arts.

Music Ministry

Leading the congregation into the presence of God through anointed worship and vocal excellence.

Media Ministry

Amplifying the Gospel through high-quality audio-visual production and digital broadcasting.

Prayer Ministry

Standing in the gap, interceding for the church, our nation, and global revival.

Welcome Ministry

Creating a warm, inviting atmosphere for all visitors and members entering the sanctuary.
