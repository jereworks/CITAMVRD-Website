Our Ministries

From children to senior citizens, CITAM Valley Road operates structured ministry tracks engineered to build holistic, Christ-like disciples.

Men Ministry

Spurring men to command generational influence, lead godly family altars, and model professional integrity.

Women Ministry

Empowering and mentoring women across all life seasons through unified biblical truths and community.

Youth Ministry

A radical community transforming culture, accelerating innovation, and growing together in Christ.

Teens Ministry

Guiding the next generation with purpose, providing mentorship during their formative years.

Children Ministry

Intentionally parenting a foundational generation through structured Sunday school and creative arts.

Music Ministry

Leading the congregation into the presence of God through anointed worship and vocal excellence.

Media Ministry

Amplifying the Gospel through high-quality audio-visual production and digital broadcasting.

Prayer Ministry

Standing in the gap, interceding for the church, our nation, and global revival.

Welcome Ministry

Creating a warm, inviting atmosphere for all visitors and members entering the sanctuary.

Hospitality Ministry

Serving with love through fellowship, meals, and caring for the practical needs of the congregation.

Counseling Ministry

Providing professional, biblical guidance to help navigate life's challenges and restore relationships.

Missions Ministry

Expanding the Kingdom of God through global evangelism, church planting, and humanitarian outreach.

CED Ministry

Deepening biblical literacy and equipping believers through structured theological education tracks.

FACE Ministry

Family & Care: Strengthening marriages, parenting, and supporting families through every stage.

Int'l Community Fellowship

Providing a home away from home for internationals and celebrating our unity in diversity.

Holy Communion Ministry

Facilitating the sacred remembrance of Christ's sacrifice with reverence and order.

Visitation & Advocacy

Extending care to the housebound and advocating for justice and social transformation.

Celebrate Recovery

A Christ-centered program helping people overcome hurts, habits, and hang-ups.

Challengers Ministry

Encouraging active faith, endurance, and pursuit of excellence in all spheres of life.

Cutting Edge Ministry

Equipping believers to remain at the forefront of influence and spiritual impact.

First Aid Ministry

Ensuring physical safety and providing immediate medical care during church services and events.

New Hope Ministry

Offering restoration, comfort, and a fresh start to those navigating difficult life circumstances.

CBF Ministry

Community Bible Fellowship: Gathering in small groups to study Scripture and foster deep connection.

CITAMValley Road

An assembly under Christ Is The Answer Ministries, established to know God and make Him known through practical discipleship structures.
